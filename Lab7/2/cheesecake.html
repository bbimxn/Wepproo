Classic Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs Brick cream cheese (Full-fat)
  • 1 cup Sugar & 1 cup Sour cream
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 2 tsp Lemon juice
  • 3 Eggs (Room temperature)

Nutrition Facts:

  • Calories: 400 kcal
  • protein: 6g
  • carbohydrates: 20g
  • fat: 34g

availability:

  • USA / Europe
  • Global
